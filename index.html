Fraeyed Reality
Anomaly spotting game

Everyone thinks Fraeymark is crazy, but he knows he sees his reality breaking. Objects moving for no reason, powered equipment acting on it's own, even strange intruders showing up. He has set up surveillance around his home and work so that he can prove the anomalous activity. Sadly, he can't seem to spot the anomalies in the cams and has asked you to poor through the footage to see what you can find. Please! Help him prove it by watching the footage and submiting the anomalies, this may be the only way Fraeymark can get the help he needs!

Clicking on these four links will bring you to four different games. The rules are easy.
1 - Enter a name fraeymark will know you by.
2 - Play the recording of the security cam footage
3 - When you see an anomaly, enter the time and anomaly type in the field
Tip: It might be easier to write the time and anomaly on paper and submit them in the end
